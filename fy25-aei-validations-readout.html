AEI Tool feedback Process improvements requested to upstream owners

01

Add dispute categories such as ATR Under Review and Invalid Dispute / User Training so cases can be triaged correctly while analysis is in progress.

02

ATR view data should be reviewed before upload with proper categorization (LDOS, ALC-EA, WPA, …) so users don't log disputes against already-known buckets.

03

Download capability should be available for all AEI users involved in analysis.

04

Improve dispute-logging UX:

  • Merge multiple ATR lines into a single dispute
  • Detailed report should respect filter / entire period
  • Bulk upload of disputes
Bottom line for leadership: Of the $2.1B disputed across 10,844 cases in FY25, $1.4B (~67%) was confirmed Valid ATR and remains in goaling. The remaining ~$700M was actively remediated through Account/Customer mapping ($196.5M), Deal/Contract exclusions ($161.8M), Asset Management cleanup ($80.2M), EA & WPA data threading ($109M), and SFC/NFP/PAYG corrections ($39.8M). $73.4M (3.5%) remains under review. Americas drives 59% of disputed value; APJC volume is small but concentrated (APJC decommission alone is $53.5M from KDDI/NTT/Samsung); EMEA shows the highest Valid-ATR rate.
